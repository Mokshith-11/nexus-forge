# Nexus Forge — Sales Kit

Everything you need to land your first client this week. Three files:

| File | What it's for |
|------|---------------|
| `lead-tracker.csv` | Your pipeline. Open in **Google Sheets** (File → Import) or Excel. |
| `outreach-scripts.md` | Copy-paste WhatsApp / email / DM scripts per niche + follow-ups. |
| `proposal-template.md` | One-page proposal to send after a call to close the deal. |

## The offer (what you're selling)
> **Free 7-day AI WhatsApp assistant pilot** — replies to leads in 10 seconds and books them automatically. If it adds value, continue at ₹15–25k/month. If not, pay nothing.

Free pilot = no risk = fast yes. You monetize after they see results.

## Daily routine (≈2 hrs/day)
1. **Add 30–40 prospects** to `lead-tracker.csv` (Google Maps, Instagram, JustDial, IndiaMART).
2. **Send 30 outreaches/day** using `outreach-scripts.md` (15 WhatsApp + 10 DM + 10 email).
3. **Follow up** everyone from 24–72h ago (most replies come on follow-up 1 & 2).
4. **Update each row's Status** so nothing slips.
5. **Book calls → run them → send `proposal-template.md` → close.**

## Status values (use these in the Status column)
`New` → `Contacted` → `Follow-up 1` → `Follow-up 2` → `Call Booked` → `Pilot Running` → `Closed-Won` / `Closed-Lost` / `Not a fit`

## This week's targets
- **120+ outreaches** across the week
- **3–5 discovery calls booked**
- **1–2 free pilots running**
- **🎯 1 paid client closed**

## Where to find prospects (India)
- **Google Maps** — search "[niche] [city]", grab name + phone + Instagram
- **Instagram** — hashtags like #[city]clinic, #[city]realestate, niche keywords
- **JustDial / IndiaMART** — phone numbers galore
- **LinkedIn** — coaches, founders, agency owners

> ⚠️ Make sure leads can reach you: confirm the website contact form delivers (test from your phone), and keep WhatsApp + email handy in every message.
