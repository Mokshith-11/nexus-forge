# Audit-Led Outreach (the highest-reply tactic)

Instead of pitching "we build AI assistants," you send a **2-minute personalized audit video** showing the prospect exactly where they're losing leads — then how you fix it. People rarely ignore free, specific advice about their own business. This works for Indian *and* international leads.

## The 4-step loop
1. **Pick a lead** from `lead-tracker.csv`.
2. **Record a 2–3 min screen video** auditing their lead flow (script below). Free tool: **Loom** (loom.com) or your phone screen recorder.
3. **Send the video** with the short DM (below).
4. **Follow up** in 2–3 days.

Target: **2 audits/day** on your top-priority leads (the big-review clinics). Quality beats volume here — one great audit can close a ₹20k/mo client.

---

## 🎥 The audit script (what to record + say)
Open their Google Business profile / Instagram / website on screen and talk through it:

1. **Intro (10s):** "Hi [Name], I looked at [Business] — really strong reputation, [X] reviews. I recorded a quick 2-min audit of how you're handling incoming leads, with 3 things that could get you more bookings."
2. **Show the gap (60s):** Open their WhatsApp/Instagram and point out:
   - "If a patient messages here at 9pm, does anyone reply instantly? Most clinics don't — and that lead often books elsewhere by morning."
   - "There's no automatic booking link — every enquiry needs manual back-and-forth."
   - "Your Instagram DMs asking about pricing/slots — who answers those, and how fast?"
3. **Show the fix (45s):** "Here's what we'd set up: an AI assistant that replies in 10 seconds, answers FAQs, and books the appointment straight into your calendar — 24/7. Here's a live example." (Show the WhatsApp demo on nexusforge.in.)
4. **Close (15s):** "If you want, I'll walk you through how this'd work for [Business] on a quick 10-min call. Reply here or message us on WhatsApp. Either way, hope the audit's useful!"

Keep it warm, specific, not salesy. You're giving value first.

## 📋 Audit checklist (what to look for)
- ⏱ Reply speed on WhatsApp/Instagram after hours
- 📅 Is there an automatic booking link, or manual only?
- 💬 Are FAQ questions (price, timing, location) answered instantly?
- 📱 Mobile experience of their website/booking
- 🔁 Do they follow up with people who enquire but don't book?
- 📊 Any lead tracking, or leads lost in the inbox?

## 💬 The DM to send with the video
> Hi [Name], I recorded a quick 2-min audit of how [Business] is handling incoming leads — spotted 3 things that could get you more bookings from the enquiries you already get. No pitch, just useful. [Loom link] — happy to explain any of it on a quick call. nexusforge.in

## ♻️ Follow-up (2–3 days later)
> Hi [Name], did the audit make sense? The WhatsApp auto-reply + booking piece is usually the quickest win — I can have it set up fast. Worth a 10-min call this week?

---

## Why this beats a plain DM
- **Specific** → proves you actually looked at their business (not mass spam).
- **Value-first** → they get free advice, so they owe you a reply.
- **Shows expertise** → the audit *is* the demo. Trust is built before the call.
- **Hard to ignore** → "I made you a video" gets opened far more than a text pitch.

Do 2 of these a day on your top clinics and you'll book calls fast.
